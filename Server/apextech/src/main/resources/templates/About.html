About ApexTech

ApexTceh is a simple product management platform built for technology retailers. It lets you browse, search, and manage electronics like laptops, phones, tablets, and accessories.

What You Can Can Do
🔍
Browse & Search

Filter products by name, brand, or category and sort by price or stock.

📦
Manage Inventory

Keep track of stock levels and pricing across your product catalog.

➕
Add Products

Quickly add new products using a validated form with brand and category selection.

How It Works
1
Browse the catalog
Head to the Products page to see everything in the system.
2
Filter & Sort
Narrow results by brand or category, then sort by name, price, or stock.
3
Add new products
Use the Add Product form to register new items with validated fields.
